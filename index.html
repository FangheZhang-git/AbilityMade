Our Story

Quality gifts featuring artwork by disabled artisans

AbilityMade was created and is currently maintained by a high school student and a college student. It is dedicated to supporting individuals with intellectual, developmental, and physical disabilities in Qingdao, China.

Although our artists face many mental and physical challenges, they have never given up. Instead, they continue to work with determination, courage, and hope. Day after day, they carefully handcraft these beautiful and meaningful pieces.

Every product on this website is 100% handmade. All proceeds from sales will go directly to our artists to support their daily lives and recognize their hard work.

We sincerely hope you enjoy this website and appreciate the creations made by our artists. Your purchase will make a real difference in their lives.

Please feel free to contact us if you have any questions or if you would like to join us in supporting this meaningful cause. We truly appreciate your kindness and support.
