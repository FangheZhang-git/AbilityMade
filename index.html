About AbilityMade

AbilityMade is a social enterprise that spotlights the talents of disabled artisans through original art pieces, handmade objects, and functional products. Each product carries an artist's profile, giving them recognition as true talents while every sale creates meaningful income and confidence.

AbilityMade also trains and supports differently-abled individuals through studio practice, retail experiences, workshops, and corporate gifting projects.

Join AbilityMade to celebrate abilities and support inclusion.

About Our Artists

AbilityMade serves and represents talented artists from inclusive studio programmes. Artists explore different media, develop their own visual styles, and turn their strengths into original work that can be shared through gifts, homeware, and meaningful merchandise.

Practicing artists, designers, and facilitators work alongside our artisans to build good work habits, refine craft skills, and celebrate each person's unique subject focus.
