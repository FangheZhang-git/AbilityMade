Our Story in Full

Made with craftsmanship, purpose, and hope

AbilityMade brings meaningful handmade creations by disabled artisans in Qingdao, China to customers everywhere.

How AbilityMade began

AbilityMade was created and is maintained by a high school student and a college student who wanted to make the work of disabled artisans more visible and accessible. This nonprofit organization supports people with intellectual, developmental, and physical disabilities in Qingdao, China.

The people behind every piece

Although facing mental and physical challenges, our artisans respond with resilience and determination. They spend hours learning techniques and carefully completing each piece by hand. Every handicraft carries the patience, skill, and individual expression of the person who made it.

Why handmade matters

Every product featured by AbilityMade is 100% handmade. From frame clutch purses and diamond paintings to beaded sculptures and bracelets, no two creations are exactly alike. These differences are part of what makes each piece meaningful.

Where your support goes

All proceeds from sales go directly to our artists. A purchase helps support their daily lives, recognizes the value of their work, and encourages them to continue creating with confidence and independence.

Be part of the story

We hope you enjoy discovering the artists’ work and the care behind it. Whether you make a purchase, share AbilityMade with someone else, or contact us to learn how you can help, your support makes a real difference.
